## Short Answer

Use the knowledge base as a writing assistant by giving it one target topic and asking it to retrieve three kinds of memory:

- topic knowledge from the AI handbook
- script/copy patterns from [[AI Tutorial Copywriting Playbook]]
- editing and psychology checks from [[AI Tutorial Editing Playbook]] and [[AI Tutorial Psychology Playbook]]

The output should be a new script, not a collage of copied source lines.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Pick one topic from the daily AI news/tutorial radar.
2. Search the vault for related tool notes, source notes, and creator ideas.
3. Choose one script format: quick win, full build, comparison, or article/thread.
4. Generate the first draft with a clear audience and proof requirement.
5. Review the draft against hook, proof, cognitive load, and CTA.
6. Save strong reusable patterns back into the copywriting playbook.

## Prompt Template

```text
你是我的 AI 教程账号文案助手。

目标选题：{topic}
目标受众：{audience}
内容形式：{short_video/article/thread/full_tutorial}
我希望观众看完后做的动作：{action}

请从知识库角度完成：
1. 提炼这个选题的真实痛点
2. 给 5 个不同类型开头钩子
3. 选择最适合的一个，写成完整文案
4. 标出每一段的作用：钩子/证明/步骤/转折/CTA
5. 给出剪辑建议和屏幕画面建议
6. 检查有没有夸张、空泛、不可验证或抄袭风险

要求：
- 不复述新闻
- 不照搬任何原文
- 必须有一个可验证结果
- 技术步骤要少而清晰
- 文案要服务于“AI 手册/工作流”定位
```

## Output Shape

The assistant should produce:

- **Positioning**: what this script is really about.
- **Hook options**: at least five.
- **Chosen draft**: the best full draft.
- **Shot list**: screen or editing suggestions.
- **Proof needed**: what screenshot, command output, demo, or metric must be shown.
- **Risk check**: unsupported claims, copied language, outdated setup, platform sensitivity.
- **Next asset**: what knowledge-base page or template this should become.

## Good Topic Examples

- "Codex 接管 Mac 到底能省哪一步"
- "把 AI 新闻采集同步进 Obsidian 图谱"
- "Kimi Work 和 Codex 的真实区别"
- "n8n 每天自动生成 AI 选题"
- "为什么很多 AI 教程看完也跑不起来"

## Bad Topic Examples

- "这个 AI 太强了"
- "最新 AI 工具大全"
- "普通人一定要学 AI"
- "一键月入过万"

These are too vague unless converted into a concrete workflow and proof.
